Our Mission

People Service Alliance (PSA) mission is to “improve the health and lives of people affected by poverty, disaster, and civil unrest. Neither the markets nor useful resource by myself can solve the problems of poverty.”

Greater than billion humans around the world lack get admission to vital items and services—from easy water and energy to a training and the freedom to take part inside the economic system.
